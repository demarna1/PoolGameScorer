Overview

Name: Average Finish

Short Description: Knockout-style game over multiple rounds.

Number of Players: This game can support 2-15 players. It is ideal for 3-8 players.

Setup

For each round, the numbers 1-15 must be issued to each player. There are two ways to do this:

  1. Texting - When starting a New Game, check the box to "Send Texts". At the start of each round, numbers will be texted to each player. Note: all players must be in your contact list.
  2. Manually
    1. Write the numbers 1-15 on pieces of paper.
    2. For the first round only, distribute the pieces evenly among players.
      • Ex: There are 5 players. Each player gets three numbers in Round 1.
      • Ex: There are 6 players. Each player gets two numbers in Round 1. The 3 extra numbers are kept hidden.
    3. For all remaining rounds, distribute any extra pieces in reverse order of finish.
      • Ex: There are 6 players. Players who finished 1-3 in the previous round get 2 numbers. Players who finished 4-6 in the previous round get 3 numbers.

Gameplay

  1. Lag to determine initial order of play.
  2. Each player keeps their numbers hidden and attempts to pocket the other player's numbered balls. The game is styled like knockout. When a player's numbered balls have been pocketed, that player must announce that they have been knocked out.
    • If a player pockets their own ball, they may continue their turn unless this has resulted in the player being knocked out.
  3. When a player has been knocked out, mark their position of finish in the app.
    • Ex: There are 6 players. Last place should be marked with a "6".
  4. The round continues until there is one player remaining. When all positions have been entered, click "Add Round" to confirm the round.
  5. The order of the next round is determined by the results of the previous round, with the winner breaking, and so on.
  6. The game continues for subsequent rounds and the app will track the average finish of players. It is often desired to agree upon a set number of rounds at the start of the game. The player with the best average finish after X rounds is declared the winner.
